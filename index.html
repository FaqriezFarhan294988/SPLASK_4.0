Some common questions
were often asked

Why is my website failing compliance checks?

Your website may not meet required standards such as accessibility, responsiveness, or proper structure based on Malaysian government guidelines.

What does the compliance score mean?

The compliance score reflects how well your website meets all required rules. A higher score means better alignment with official standards.

Why are some elements marked as errors?

Errors appear when your website does not follow specific compliance rules such as missing tags, poor structure, or invalid components.

How do I start a compliance scan?

Simply enter your website URL in the input field and click “Scan Website.” The system will automatically analyze your site.

Can I improve my score after scanning?

Yes. SPLaSK provides recommendations so you can fix issues and rescan your website to achieve a better score.
